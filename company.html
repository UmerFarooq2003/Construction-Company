CEO of United Engineering Associates

Qazi Farooq Ahmed, a visionary leader, is the esteemed CEO of United Engineering Associates, bringing with him a remarkable experience of over 26 years in the construction industry. With a profound expertise in the field, he has demonstrated exceptional leadership skills throughout his 20-year tenure as the owner of the company. Graduating from UET in 1994, Mr. Farooq Ahmed achieved an impressive 9th position in the Merit List, showcasing his exceptional academic capabilities.

Having initially worked with renowned organizations, he embarked on a remarkable journey in the year 2000 by establishing United Engineering Associates (UEA). Since then, he has steered the company towards unprecedented success and growth. UEA has successfully delivered over 500 major projects within a span of 20-23 years, making it a trusted name in the industry.

With a notable presence in the C2 firms of PEC (Pakistan Engineering Council), United Engineering Associates has played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of various sectors. The company has made significant contributions to the development of remarkable projects such as medical colleges, villages, and hospitals, leaving a positive impact on communities.

Under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Farooq Ahmed, United Engineering Associates continues to strive for excellence, embracing innovative approaches and delivering outstanding results.
